Wait, Varsity has its own coding team?

That's right!

What do you guys do?

The V coders get to build interactive projects for Varsity, from the V-MBTI quiz, to interactive praise report walls at V camp. We're open to build all sorts of interactions for V!

Why does V Coders exist?

V Coders aims to be a community where coders in Varsity can gather together, and use their giftings for His Kingdom!

Do I need to know how to code to join?

Yes and no. If you can't code, but you are currently studying in a coding related course such as Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or Information Systems, we highly encourage you to join us! Let's sharpen our skills together!
